Project Description

The project was created during a Web Application Development (COMP 229) course at Centennial College using Node.JS and Angular.

FAQ

How can I add a new survey?

You must register an account to add new surveys. Once registered, click on the New Survey button on the survey list pages.

How can I see the results for my survey?

You can access the results by logging in and viewing your survey list.

What happened to my survey results?

If you edit your survey, it will delete any previously saved results/statistics.
